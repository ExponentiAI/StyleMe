Fashion dataset.
In this fashion dataset, PublicClothing and PublicClothing2 correspond to RGB images of clothing crawled from different clothing websites twice. 
The RGB folder contains clothing RGB pictures manually downloaded by three fashion designers from the clothing image website, corresponding to clothRGB 1, clothRGB 2, and clothRGB 3 respectively. 
The Sketch folder contains sketches manually drawn by three fashion designers according to their downloaded clothing pictures, corresponding to clothsketch1, clothsketch2, and clothsketch3 respectively.
